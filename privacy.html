Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026

ChainGuard is built with privacy in mind. We don't require email registration, we don't sell your data, and we don't track you across the web. Your wallet address is used only to provide the service.

1. What We Collect

ChainGuard collects minimal data to operate the service:

  • Wallet addresses — when you connect your wallet or paste a contract address, we use this to fetch on-chain data and provide analysis. We do not store wallet addresses permanently.
  • Usage data — we track the number of scans performed per wallet address to enforce free plan limits. This counter resets daily and is stored in memory only.
  • Transaction data — if you upgrade to Pro, we verify your payment by reading public on-chain transaction data. No financial information is stored by us.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • Email addresses or personal information
  • Private keys or seed phrases (we never ask for these)
  • Browsing history or cross-site tracking data
  • Device fingerprints or persistent cookies
  • IP addresses for identification purposes

3. Third-Party Services

ChainGuard uses the following third-party APIs to provide its service:

  • GoPlus Security API — for smart contract security analysis
  • Etherscan API — for on-chain token and transaction data

When you scan a contract or wallet, the address is sent to these services. Please review their privacy policies for more information on how they handle data.

4. Wallet Connection

When you connect your wallet through MetaMask or another Web3 wallet, ChainGuard only requests read access to your public wallet address. We never request permission to sign transactions, transfer funds, or access private keys. The connection is read-only.

5. Data Retention

ChainGuard does not maintain a persistent database of user data. Scan counters are stored in memory and reset when the server restarts or after 24 hours. Pro plan activations are stored in memory for the duration of the subscription period.

6. Cookies

ChainGuard does not use tracking cookies. We may use session storage in your browser temporarily to maintain your wallet connection state within a single session.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data and do not maintain persistent user records, there is no personal data to request deletion of. If you have concerns, contact us through our official X (Twitter) account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
